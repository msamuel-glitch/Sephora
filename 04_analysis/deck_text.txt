RFM Segment
Segmentation based on 
Recency
, Frequency
 and Amount.
This is computed on each end of month. It means that for each 
transaction made, we are taking into consideration the 
segmentation computed of the end of the previous month.
Ex: purchase made on the 12th of June will be associated with the RFM 
segmentation from the end of May.
Here is the graphic representation of the segment rules.
As a consequence
, customers with RFM 1, 2,3 are more valuable 
and engaged to Sephora than the others.
