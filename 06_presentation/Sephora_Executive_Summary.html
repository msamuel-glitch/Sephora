The Explorer Index

Age does not predict discovery; wallet does. The highest-spending Baby Boomer has an Explorer Index of 0.671, making them vastly more exploratory than a low-spending Gen Z customer (0.063).

The Explorer Index reveals that brand discovery intent crosses generational lines entirely. Your most valuable discovery segment is defined by spend decile and purchase cadence.

Four Honest Limitations

  • Sequential Journeys: Need to map sequence over three visits to maximize lifetime value.
  • Consideration Set: Occasion-level routing required to avoid brand cannibalisation.
  • Lapsed Customers: System learns from active customers; maximum untargeted revenue remains unmodeled.
  • Seasonal Regime: December gifting mechanics require a dedicated seasonal variant.
