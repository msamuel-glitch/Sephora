THE SYSTEM / 04

How the system
finds the signal

12 million possible customer-brand combinations. 50,805 customers × 239 brands. We scored 2.37 million meaningful ones. Here is how.

Route 1
The Basket

What customers buy together

45,446 baskets analyzed. Pairs appearing together 50% more than chance entered the candidate pool.

Route 2
The Twin

Who buys like you

50,805 customer profiles compared. Brands bought by your behavioral twin become your candidates.

Route 3
The Category

Where you already spend

Every customer has a dominant axis: makeup, skincare, fragrance, haircare. Unpurchased brands in that axis enter the pool.

Route 4
The New Arrival

Brands with no history yet

New brands have no basket data. We map them to their closest established proxy and inherit that proxy's audience.

TEST

Three systems.
One test.

We locked away October–December data. Built everything on January–September only. Then asked three systems to predict what customers would buy in the holdout window. P@3 = of every 3 recommendations made, how many were correct.
Popularity Engine — the floor
4.02%

Recommends the most-purchased brands for everyone. Any intelligent system must beat this.

Hybrid Engine — discovery layer
0.52%

Lower raw accuracy by design. Built for discovery, not prediction. It surfaces brands the ML model would never recommend.

ML Model — the predictor
5.26%

31% more accurate than the popularity baseline. 1.24 percentage points above the floor. Trained on 9 months of real purchase data.
