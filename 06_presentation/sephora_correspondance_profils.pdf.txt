For each product axis × brand combination: complete list of buyer profiles (gender · generation · current axis · current brand · channel)
— 894 rules · 95 distinct next-product/brand combinations
How to read this document
Each section below represents one target product × brand combination — i.e. a product that customers are predicted to buy next. Under each
header you will find the complete list of buyer profiles: every combination of gender, generation, current product axis, current brand and channel
that statistically leads to that next purchase. The probability % is the share of transitions from that profile that land on this product/brand. The
expected basket is the predicted transaction value. Profiles are sorted by number of observed transitions (strongest signal first).
